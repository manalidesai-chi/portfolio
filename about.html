About

I’m a growing maker of many types always ready to learn, try and fail at something new. I’m passionate about maker education and socially engaged design. Also cats. I love cats.

As a proud Chicagoan I grew up being resourceful and always making things. I make things rough, cheap and fast, then iterate on that. I thrive in fun interdisciplinary environments.

Philosophy

Reflection

I don’t think most of us do enough of this, myself included. I’m a firm believer that through reflection and shared understanding we could solve a lot of the world's problems and make ourselves better as a whole. From a group spat, product design, user research, creativity and open mindedness reflection fosters long term growth. I think design should facilitate reflection and not just consumption to bring value to our lives.

Manali Desai © 2020
