Works

Anti-Couch Potato, Potato

Designed and created a fully functional prototype of a large sweet potato that, like a pet, requires periodic walks otherwise plays annoying music and makes snarky comments. Uses machine learning and intertial tracking to detect whether the potato is being walked or not. Here is the Research Paper and Video

Foster Portal

Inspired by my experience fostering cats, prototyped an app in Figma for local animal shelter to facilitate passive communication on foster health between fosters and directors to improve crisis management and reduce burden on directors, as part of Si482. Try it out here.

Psuedo Pocket Scratcher

Uses inertial tracking of the phones movement to program geastures that dynamically manipulate(volume, pseudo scratch effects, on/off) digital audio live, inspired by record scratching. Here's a quick demo video.

The Nut Buster and Nut Ninja

Created a food processor controlled with a stress ball and 3D attachments to a nut cracker so you can crack nut nut with your mouth. Wrote the Nutjob's Guide to Shelling out your Frustrations as a process manual for using them to reflect on and let go of your frustrations.
