Works

Anti-Couch Potato, Potato

Designed and created a fully functional prototype of a large sweet potato that, like a pet, requires periodic walks otherwise plays annoying music and makes snarky comments. Uses machine learning and intertial tracking to detect wether the potato is walking or not. Research paper, program and more info to be added soon.

Foster Portal

Inspired by my experience fostering cats, prototyped an app in Figma for local animal shelter to facilitate passive communication on foster health between fosters and directors to improve crisis management and reduce burden on directors, as part of Si482.

The Nut Buster and Nut Ninja

Created a food processor controlled with a stress ball and 3D attachments to a nut cracker so you can crack nut nut with your mouth. Wrote the Nutjob's Guide to Shelling out your Frustrations as a process manual for using them to reflect on and let go your frustrations.

Flip the Bean

Queried Facebook Bean event data and made a word cloud of the names sized based on the number of people interested in the event
